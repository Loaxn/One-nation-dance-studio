Hello! My name is Loana Chalach, and I am a first-year multimedia student

As someone who has always had a deep love and appreciation for dance, I decided to create this website for a dance school.

Combining my passion for dance and my growing skills in web development, I wanted to provide a platform that promotes the art of dance and its transformative power.

Through this website, I aim to share the beauty of dance and inspire others to explore the world of movement and self-expression. It has been an exciting journey for me as a budding multimedia student to create this platform, and I hope it serves as a valuable resource for dancers and dance enthusiasts alike.
